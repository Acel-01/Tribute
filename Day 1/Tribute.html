If not for Bill Gates, I probably wouldn't even know how to ctrl x and ctrl v by now!

William Henry Gates III popularly known as Bill Gates was born on the 28th of October, 1995. He was born and raised in Seattle, Washington and is of American nationality. From a family of five with an elder and a younger sister.

Gates is the Co-founder of Microsoft, pioneer of first the PC-DOS, MS-DOS and then the line of Windows operating systems used worldwide. He has been the richest man on earth eighteen times from 1995 to 2017 and has been second richest since then till date.

From Bill's popular quotes, you'll easily notice that he is a man doesn't dwell on success or loudmouthed about his wealt, but is someone always looking out for his mistakes or failure with the mindset of learning from them. Gates is a philanthropist to the latter always willing to help the poor, sick and needy. He with his wife started the "Bill and Melinda Gates foundation" which today is the biggest charity foundation today. In June 2006, he transitioned to a part-time role at his multi-billionaire company, Microsoft so he could work full-time at the private charitable foundation he opened with his wife in the year 2000.

Some, if not most look at Bill Gates today and believe his life was a smooth drive with no potholes here and there but every influential person today has had a taste of failure and that's just a fact that need not be argued. Bill suffered bullying from his mates when he was younger cause he had a small stature compared to his peers. Also, he has faced rejection from companies while applying to build softwares for them.

Up till now, you've read more about who William Henry Gates III is and less about the impact he has made on me and how he has influenced my life choices. It may be true that we haven't met before, he probably doesn't even know I exist, but his works alone inspired my desire to delve into the software world. Developing applications,games, web applications, websites, etc became something I wanted to at least have I idea about because of him and this decision had been made since I was in JSS1. Seeing the applications Microsoft made more popular over time, solve real life problems, encourages me to go on, push forward, and keep learning even when I feel tired with the hope that I can do something better, contribute my own quota to make life easier for everyone.

Over time, I have chosen to take programming courses to aid me in this path. One of them is choosing to learn Front end developement under Engineering Career Expo also known as ECX.

As the years count on, I hope to be a full time specialist in the field of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning and also contribute to the betterment of humanity.
